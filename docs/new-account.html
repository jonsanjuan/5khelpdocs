New Account

When your administrator creates an account for you, you will receive an email containing a link to initiate your account.

When you click the link, you will be be prompted to set a password. Enter your password, and click Submit.

The first time you log in, you will be prompted to agree to a license agreement before continuing. Check the box, and click Accept Terms & Conditions to continue.
